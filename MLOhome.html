The Magical Lucan Open is one of Ireland’s premier amateur golf tournaments, celebrated for its competitive spirit and scenic setting at Lucan Golf Club, located just 40 minutes from Dublin city.

Looking ahead, the 2025 Magical Lucan Open promises to be an even more exciting event, drawing golf enthusiasts from across the country. With dates set for 30th June to 6th July, 2025, players and spectators alike can expect an incredible display of talent, as the tournament continues to build on its legacy of excellence.

The 2024 MLO saw a fiercely contested final, with Kevin Gormley securing his victory in dramatic fashion with an eagle on the 72nd hole, further cementing his reputation as one of Ireland’s top amateur golfers.

The event also enjoyed tremendous support from the public, with over 5,000 spectators attending throughout the week, creating a fantastic atmosphere and further establishing the Magical Lucan Open as one of the most anticipated tournaments in the amateur golf calendar.
